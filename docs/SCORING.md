# Collection scoring (MVP)

Your **Collection Score** is a number from **0 to 100**. It is the sum of five parts. Each part has a maximum. Nothing is hidden: the same rules run for everyone.

---

## 1. Variety (max 25 points)

**What it is:** How many **different scent families** you have (e.g. Fresh, Woody, Floral, Oriental).

**How it’s scored:**  
- 1 family → 5 points  
- 2 families → 10 points  
- 3 families → 15 points  
- 4 families → 20 points  
- 5 or more families → 25 points  

**Plain English:** More variety in scent types gives more points. One-note collections score low; mixed families score high.

---

## 2. Versatility (max 20 points)

**What it is:** How many bottles work in **many situations** (e.g. all-season or day-to-night).

**How it’s scored:**  
- 0 versatile bottles → 0 points  
- 1 versatile bottle → 5 points  
- 2 versatile bottles → 10 points  
- 3 or more versatile bottles → 20 points  

**Plain English:** Bottles that you can wear in different seasons or from day to evening improve your score.

---

## 3. Season coverage (max 20 points)

**What it is:** Whether you have something suitable for **each season** (spring, summer, fall, winter).

**How it’s scored:**  
- 5 points per season you cover.  
- 4 seasons → 20 points, 3 → 15, 2 → 10, 1 → 5, 0 → 0.

**Plain English:** The more seasons you’re covered for, the higher this part of the score.

---

## 4. Occasion coverage (max 20 points)

**What it is:** Whether you have something suitable for **each occasion** we care about: office, casual, date night, formal.

**How it’s scored:**  
- 5 points per occasion you cover.  
- 4 occasions → 20 points, 3 → 15, 2 → 10, 1 → 5, 0 → 0.

**Plain English:** Covering more situations (work, weekend, date, formal) increases this part.

---

## 5. Low redundancy (max 15 points)

**What it is:** We look at **“roles”**: same scent family and same set of occasions. Bottles that share the same role are redundant.

**How it’s scored:**  
- Start at 15 points.  
- For each **extra** bottle that duplicates an existing role (same category + same occasions), we subtract 5 points.  
- Minimum for this part is 0.

**Plain English:** Fewer duplicates (same type, same use) keep this part high; lots of overlap lowers it.

---

## Total score

**Total = Variety + Versatility + Season coverage + Occasion coverage + Low redundancy**

- Each part is capped at its maximum.  
- The total is capped at **100**.

So: **variety** (different families), **versatility** (all-rounders), **season coverage**, **occasion coverage**, and **low redundancy** together define the score. The rules are the same for everyone and can be shown in the app (e.g. as a breakdown).
